A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus. It takes Venus longer to rotate once on its axis than it does to complete a full orbit around the Sun. So if you lived there, you'd celebrate New Year's before you ever saw a sunrise. Also you'd be dead because it's 900°F and rains sulfuric acid, but still — fun calendar situation.
